# Responsible Gaming Disclaimer

**Drive For Good is a skill-based competition platform combined with charitable giving. Please play responsibly.**

## Important Disclaimers

### Not a Gambling Platform
Drive For Good is designed around real golf performance tracking and monthly draws. Participation is voluntary and tied to an authentic subscription service with direct charitable impact.

### No Guaranteed Returns
Monthly draws are based on your submitted golf scores. There is no guarantee of winning. Prizes are distributed only based on matching score patterns drawn.

### Subscription Commitment
Subscriptions are recurring and charged automatically. Users must actively manage their subscription and can cancel anytime.

### Charitable Contribution
A minimum of 10% of every subscription is directed to qualified charities. This is a core feature of the platform and cannot be waived.

### Responsible Use
- Only users 18+ may participate.
- Do not spend more than you can afford.
- Do not submit false or fraudulent scores.
- If you experience issues with platform use, contact support@driveforgood.app.

### Dispute Resolution
All disputes regarding winnings, scores, or payouts are subject to Drive For Good's dispute resolution process. Decisions made by our verification team are final.

### Player Support
If you feel overwhelmed or would like to take a break:
- You can pause or cancel your subscription anytime
- Reach out to our support team: support@driveforgood.app
- Resources for healthy gaming habits available upon request

---

**Play responsibly. Give generously. Compete fairly.**
